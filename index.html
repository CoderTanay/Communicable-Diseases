Strep Throat, By: Tanay Katyal

More Information

Definition:

A Strep Throat is a sore throat with fever caused by a group of bacteria which are called Streptococcus.

Causes:

You get strep throat from droplets. You can get this when you are near someone who is affected; and they talk, sneeze, or cough near you. This happens since when you do any of those three things you release droplets from your mouth or nose.

Facts

  1. Doctors swab you throat to see if you have strep throat.
  2. People with strep throat should stay home and take antibiotics until they no longer have a fever.
  3. People can spread strep throat by talking, coughing, or sneezing since it spreads through droplets.

Prevention

  1. You should cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
  2. You should throw away and not reuse a used tissue a second time.
  3. You should wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
